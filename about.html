Hi there! My name is Ali and welcome to my learning journal

After several months of learning Frontend Developement by myself, I've made the big jump over to Scrimba to learn from professionals, create innovative projects and meet like-minded peers.

How I stay committed to learning

I always consider learning as a way of improving my skills and staying ahead of the curve, while for some people learning is a chore, for me it is one of the most purposeful things a person can do. This is exactly what motivates me to keep improving and keep learning.

How I got started

I started simple and gradually started learning Frontend Developement. I would take notes about what I was learning. After each learning session, I'd use my notes to not only reflect on what I've learned, but apply it on projects to get hands on practice.
